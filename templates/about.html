About HN Live

HN Live is an alternative interface to Hacker News which streams the data from Hacker News in real time. Though HN isn't updated terribly frequently, having a site that shows the data in real time is rather interesting, for me anyway. I am also too lazy to reload the page every time I want to see what's new.
